Our Mission

        The City Hoops Institute utilizes basketball to promote development and peace among youth, young adults, and communities by ensuring developmental assets are available to enhance life skills, core competencies, and whole potential. 

Information

The City Hoops Institute provides much more beyond basketball resources. We believe a wide range of academic, athletic, health, and wellness consulting services and resources should be available and affordable to all individuals, organizations, and communities of all ages and types to help them achieve their whole potential in multiple environments of society.

Utilizing various aspects of basketball and other sports to transfer life skills, the CHI specializes in individual, organizational, & community development by ensuring access to particular internal and external assets.

Positive Development

The City Hoops Institute focuses on positive development to build life skills through sport, for students, athletes, and others to achieve their whole potential, but to also ease community tension, to decrease conflict and to enhance decision making abilities in multiple environments, such as before or after school, at home, or in the community.

CHI has no borders.

The CHI values diversity and collaboration because basketball unites all of us around the globe. 

While we strive to impact our local neighborhoods, we also have international opportunities that exist among the CHI's vast network of coaches, players, teams, programs, and institutions.

We would love to partner with you as well!  
Please contact the CHI for further information.

As an Illinois not for profit organization, the CHI aims to raise developmental resources for disadvantaged individuals and communities. A portion of all proceeds and ALL of which is generously donated to the CHI is contributed to providing before or after school recreational, or educational services, resources, supplies, or opportunities to students of the Chicago Public Schools, the Archdiocese of Chicago, or various charter networks of schools throughout the Chicagoland area, or the Chicago Park District. Please contact the City Hoops Institute for details and for additional information on supporting sport for development and peace, or to donate time, resources, or support our youth development and community programming efforts.

For additional information on our community efforts and individual or group services, please contact the City Hoops Institute.
