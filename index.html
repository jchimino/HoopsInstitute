Information

The City Hoops Institute provides health, wellness, and athletic consulting services, resources, and experience to individuals, organizations, and communities to help them achieve their whole potential as contributors to multiple domains of society. Utilizing sport to transfer life skills, the CHI aims to positively impact students, athletes, coaches, and participants of all ages by ensuring their access to developmental assets. To learn more about services, opportunities and the benefits to enrolling in the City Hoops Institute, sign up below.

Positive Development

The City Hoops Institute focuses on positive development to build life skills through sport and to ease community tension, conflict, or poor decision making in multiple domains, such as in academia, at home, in the community, or in the office.  If you're a coach, an educator, student, parent, manager or person inerested in sport for development and peace, please sign up below to request more information about our impact on students, athletes, and their community.
